Features — How it works

Login into your Account

After a brief registration you will be able to login into your unique account and save your data safely on our database for you to be accessible online on any iOS device.

Collect your Data

Digit your Blood Sugar Level (BSL) as soon as you launch the APP on your iPhone. You can also select your favourite unit between mg/dl and mmol. As soon as you digit your first BSL your records will be updated and a line chart will be populated * to give you a glimpse of your BSLs flow.

* In order to generate a precise chart,
the software requires at least 4 records.

Check your BSL History

Tap on the History or on the Line Chart in the Homepage to access your BSL record history and a more detailed Line chart. You can also add comments such as administered insulin, food intake etc... All of your comments will be stored safely on our database.
